/datum/say_channel/global_channel

Global say channel datums act as optional parters to delimited say channels, with the delimited channel passing all messages sent over it, irrespective of subchannel or range, to the global channel. In turn, any message sent to the global channel will be sent to all listeners on the delimited channel, irrespective of subchannel or range.

Vars

delimited_channelThis global channel datum's partner delimited channel datum. All messages sent through this channel will be sent to every listener on the delimited channel.
delimited_channel_idThe channel ID of this global channel's partner delimited channel.
